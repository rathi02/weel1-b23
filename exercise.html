Puchong

Puchong is a major town and a parliamentary constituency in the southern portion Petaling Region, Selangor Malaysia.It is bordered by Subang Jaya in the north, Sepang and Putrajaya in the south, Serdang in the east and Putra Heights in the west.

The first settlers of the area were the orang asli community in a village known as Kampung PulasHowever it is believed that area was explored by immigrants fron Sumatra and Java.

Puchong has both local and foreign banks. There is a wet market called Puteri Mart and the town parkwhere residents can shopof their groceries
